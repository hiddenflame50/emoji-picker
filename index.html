1. Introduction

Welcome to Emoji Picker Pro. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our Chrome extension. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Local Storage Data

Our extension stores the following information locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Recent Emojis: The last 20 emojis you've used for quick access
  • User Preferences: Your selected emoji style (Native, Apple, Google, Facebook, X)
  • Skin Tone Selection: Your preferred skin tone for applicable emojis
  • Theme Preference: Your choice between light and dark mode
  • Category State: Your last selected emoji category and search queries

2.2 No Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information such as:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Browsing history or website data
  • Location data
  • Passwords or sensitive information
  • Information from other websites or applications

3. How We Use Your Information

The locally stored information is used exclusively to:

  • Provide quick access to your recently used emojis
  • Remember your preferred emoji style and skin tone settings
  • Maintain your theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Restore your last emoji category when reopening the extension
  • Enhance your overall user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage Only

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

4.2 Data Encryption

Chrome automatically encrypts extension data stored locally, providing an additional layer of security for your preferences.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. Since all data remains on your local device, there is no external data sharing whatsoever.

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 Emoji Images

When using non-native emoji styles (Apple, Google, Facebook, X), the extension loads emoji images from content delivery networks (CDNs). These requests are made directly by your browser and do not include any personal information.

6.2 No Analytics

We do not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, or data collection tools. Your usage patterns remain completely private.

7. User Rights and Data Control

7.1 Data Access

You have full control over your data. All preferences can be viewed and modified through the extension interface.

7.2 Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Clearing recent emojis through the extension interface
  • Resetting preferences to default settings
  • Uninstalling the extension (removes all local data)
  • Using Chrome's extension data management tools

8. Children's Privacy

Our extension is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special privacy concerns regarding children under 13 years of age.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any updates will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.

10. Permissions Explanation

Our extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab: To insert emojis into the currently active webpage
  • scripting: To execute the emoji insertion functionality
  • storage: To save your preferences and recent emojis locally
  • tabs: To open donation/support links when requested

11. Data Retention

Your data is retained locally until you choose to clear it or uninstall the extension. There is no automatic data deletion or expiration.
